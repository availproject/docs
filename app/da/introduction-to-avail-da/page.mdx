# Introduction to Avail DA

Avail DA delivers verifiable data availability that lets every chain launch faster, scale further, and reach more users. 

Avail DA guarantees data integrity through a combination of 
- Validity proofs
- KZG polynomial commitments
- Data availability sampling (DAS)
- Light client verification
