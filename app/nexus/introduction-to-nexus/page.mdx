# Introduction to Avail Nexus

*Avail Nexus is the fastest, and easiest way for your application to go crosschain üåä*
---


 Avail Nexus is a meta-interoperability protocol that unifies users, assets, apps, and chains, letting developers **build once, scale everywhere**, and access liquidity and users from across the Web3 economy.

**Deposit, Swap, Stake, Bridge, Pay** - Avail Nexus abstracts chains and balances away so that *"intent"* replaces complexity.

A seamless ***‚Äúbridgeless‚Äù*** experience. 

## Integrate Nexus (Choose your path)

Get started building your Liquid App today by integrating Nexus.
- **Nexus Elements**: A plug and play, ready to use components for almost any use case. Use as is or customise and go to market fast.
- **Nexus SDK**: Headless SDK with more granular features and control for creating truly custom and integrated experiences.
