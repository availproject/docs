**Avail Nexus is the fastest, and easiest way for your application to go crosschain.**

 It is a meta-interoperability protocol that unifies users, assets, apps, and chains, letting developers build once, scale everywhere, and access liquidity and users from across the Web3 economy.

- Unifies assets across chains
- Executes intents across ecosystems
- Handles gas, routing, and verification under the hood

**Deposit, Swap, Transfer, Bridge** - Avail Nexus abstracts chains away so that *"intent"* replaces complexity.

A seamless ***“bridgeless”*** experience. 

## Integrate Nexus (Choose your path)

Get started building your Liquid App today by integrating Nexus.

- **Nexus Elements**: A plug and play, ready to use UI components for almost any use case. Use as is or customise and go to market fast.
- **Nexus SDK**: Headless SDK with more granular features and control for creating truly custom and integrated experiences.
